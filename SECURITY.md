# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report security vulnerabilities privately to:

- `sprout@pruningmypothos.com`

When reporting, include:

- Affected component (for example: `apps/api`, `apps/web`, `workspace-mcp`)
- Clear reproduction steps
- Expected impact and severity
- Any proof-of-concept material that helps us verify quickly

## Response Expectations

- Initial acknowledgment target: within 2 business days
- Triage and severity assessment target: within 5 business days
- Remediation timeline depends on severity and operational risk

## Disclosure Guidance

- Please do not open public issues for unpatched vulnerabilities.
- We will coordinate disclosure timing after a fix or mitigation is available.

## Scope

This policy applies to this repository, including:

- Soothsayer app (`apps/web`, `apps/api`, `apps/worker`)
- Workspace MCP kernel (`workspace-mcp`)
